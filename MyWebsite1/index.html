Welcome

Ecars is an electric car company with the mission of successfully increase the number of electric cars used in the real world. Here at Ecars we use various software that would make driving much easier and more fun. We are certain that this will decrease the percentage of gas and diesel cars used in everyday life. Our goal is to protect the environment. Ecars vehicles are produced in three factories located in, Illinois, California, and Massachusetts. Another goal of Ecars is to have one of the safest factories in the world, to make this one of our many achievements Ecars is taking a proactive approach to safety, making sure all employees attend a multi-day training program for at least one month before ever setting foot past the factory doors.
