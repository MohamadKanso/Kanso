About Ecar

Ecar was founded in 2020 by a group of wise engineers that wanted to prove that electric vehicles can be more comfortable and quicker than gasoline cars. Ecar builds not only all-electric vehicles but we also assure scalable clean energy generation and storage products. Ecar would like to make sure that the world finally realises that relying less on fossil fuels and moving on to new and advanced technology will save the planet and allow us to live more comfortably. Here at Ecars we use various software that would make driving much easier and more fun.

Ecar produces many vehicles all around the world, currently we have got four factories, one of our many plans is to start building more factories around the world increase the rate of Ecars being built. Ecar also works with many battery companies as we would like to improve all our battery efficiencies even more enuring that each car has an increase in miles. This is just the beginning for Ecar, as we are the most affordable electric car company in the world we will ensure to make electric cars the new normal.
